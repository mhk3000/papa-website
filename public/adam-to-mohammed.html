Karbala Prophet Adam AS to Prophet Mohammed SA & Ali SA

Posted on Wed, Jul 17, 2024 Karbala Aimat Tahereen

Moharrum Discourse of Syedna Burhanuddin (1991 English, 1412 Hijri)

  1. Prophet Adam AS was expelled from Paradise and fell ill. He visited Karbala, stumbled, and bled. He asked Angel Gabriel the reason for this. Gabriel replied, “One of your descendants, Hussain AS, will be martyred here.” Adam asked, “Who will kill him?” Gabriel answered, “Shimr.” Adam cursed Shimr. Syedna AQ says: O believers, you are the descendants of Adam. You should mourn for Hussain Imam SA.

Moharrum Discourse of Syedna Burhanuddin (2012)

  1. Prophet Adam AS came to Karbala and stumbled, bleeding. Adam asked Angel Gabriel why this happened. Gabriel replied, “This is where one of your descendants, Hussain AS, will be killed.” Adam asked, “Who will be his enemy?” Gabriel answered, “His name is Shimr.” Adam cursed Shimr. Syedna AQ says: O believers, you are the descendants of Adam. You should mourn for Hussain Imam SA.
  2. Prophet Noah AS went to Karbala in his ark, facing waves. Noah asked Angel Gabriel why the waves came. Gabriel replied, “Here, the family of Prophet Mohammad SA will be wronged and face waves of tyranny. Hussain and his male family members and children will be martyred.” Noah AS mourned.
  3. Prophet Abraham SA passed through Karbala and fell from his horse, bleeding. Abraham asked Gabriel the reason. Gabriel replied, “This is Karbala, where Hussain SA will be martyred.” Abraham asked, “How will he be martyred?” Gabriel answered, “By the people of Islam with the sword of Islam.” Abraham mourned.
  4. Prophet Moses AS came to Karbala with his successor Yushea AS. Aaron AS had passed away. Moses stumbled and bled. Moses asked Gabriel the reason. Gabriel replied, “This is the place of Hussain’s martyrdom.” Moses mourned.
  5. Prophet Jesus SA reached Karbala and saw deers crying. Jesus asked Angel Gabriel the reason. Gabriel replied, “This is the place where the son of Fatima, Hussain, will be martyred.” Jesus AS mourned for Hussain SA.
  6. Angel Gabriel came to Prophet SA while Hussain Imam SA was playing in his lap. Gabriel said, “You love your grandson, but he will be martyred.” Prophet SA asked, “Where?” Gabriel replied, “In Karbala.” Gabriel brought the soil of Karbala and gave it to Prophet SA, who smelled it and gave it to his wife Umme Salma. He said, “When this soil turns red, know that my grandson Hussain SA has been martyred in Karbala.” Umme Salma was to mourn then.
  7. Imam Hussain SA was martyred. Prophet SA appeared in Umme Salma’s dream with a dusty face. She asked, “What is the matter?” Prophet SA replied, “My Hussain SA was martyred in Karbala. I have come from there. See the soil.” Umme Salma saw the soil had turned red. She spread a net mate on the ground, called the ladies, and started mourning for Hussain Imam SA.
  8. Maula Ali SA, returning from the Battle of Jamal, met a Christian monk and asked if there was any water spring. The monk pointed out a place. Maula Ali removed a stone, and water gushed out. Ali SA said, “From where the spring came out, my Hussain SA and his family will be martyred.” Ali SA and his companions mourned.

After the martyrdom of Hussain, Asbag bin Nabata found a bone at the place Maula Ali had pointed out. It was the same place where Hussain was martyred.
