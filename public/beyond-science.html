My Prophet Mohammed

Beyond Science

Posted on Fri, Sep 6, 2024 Duat Mutlaqeen Mohammed Burhanuddin Observation

1. The Healing of Ruksana

This story is about Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin (RA), the 52nd Dai al-Fatemi of the Shia Dawoodi Bohra community. In 1970, a young man, about 19 years old at the time, had a 15-year-old sister named Ruksana who was unable to urinate. The doctors at Prince Aga Khan Hospital advised immediate dialysis, which was not a common procedure then.

In distress, the man went to Raudat Tahera, where Syedna was present, and narrated his situation. Syedna asked the name of his sister, and upon hearing it was Ruksana, instructed the man to call her Fatema from that moment on. When he returned to the hospital, something miraculous occurred: Ruksana began urinating, much to the doctor’s surprise. The doctor asked where the man had been, and upon hearing the story of Syedna's blessing, he confirmed that Ruksana was fine and discharged her.

2. A Brush with Death

The same man, now narrating another miracle, was around 40 years old when this incident took place. One night, after leaving Badri Mahal around midnight, he decided to walk towards VT Station after a bus didn’t arrive. As he walked, a Number 9 bus pulled up and stopped specifically for him. The bus, driven recklessly, didn’t stop at usual stops. At the Cotton Exchange, it collided with another bus.

Miraculously, though he was seated near the window, the man found himself safe, while passengers in front were severely injured. He was wearing white prayer clothes and a white prayer mat, given by Syedna Burhanuddin (RA). In the morning, he narrated the event to Syedi Mazoon Saheb Qutbuddin (later the 53rd Dai), who instructed him to repeat the story at night. Upon hearing it, Syedi Mazoon Saheb told the man to perform charity for three days. The Miya Saheb (mosque priests) who were present explained that Syedi Mazoon Saheb had perceived the “death” written on the man’s forehead and, by instructing him to perform charity twice, had given him a second life.

3. Miracle in Surat

During a majlis (religious gathering) in Surat, Syedna Burhanuddin (RA) was approached by a man with his 12-year-old daughter who was both deaf and mute. The father sought Syedna's help. Maula, with his unique compassion, asked the girl to say "Yes (نعم)." In that moment, the girl spoke for the first time and began to hear. This transformation left everyone in awe, witnessing Syedna’s ability to heal.

These stories, shared by those who experienced them, reveal Syedna Burhanuddin’s profound spiritual impact on his followers, as he provided healing and guidance beyond scientific explanation.
